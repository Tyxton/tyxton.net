Japanese Rock

Rock is a very broad genre of music and spans it influence over almost all of the other genres. Japanese rock, in particular my tastes in the music, seem to be more... raw, more representative of some of my deeper feelings. This kind of rock helps me feel my highest highs as well as my lowest lows, I owe a lot of joy to the music I've listened to over the years.
